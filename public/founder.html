The Journey

From Vision to Reality

Rachna Gupta is a serial entrepreneur and digital commerce strategist who started her entrepreneurial journey in 2017 with a mission to make quality fashion accessible to every Indian woman.

She founded Secret Attire, a women-focused social commerce apparel brand that has delivered lakhs of orders across India, and later launched WSBulk, a digital marketing solutions company offering WhatsApp automation, performance marketing, and growth tools for startups and SMEs.

Her latest venture, LYVSTOR, is an AI-powered live-stream shopping platform under LIVSTO Apparels OPC Private Limited, designed to help social sellers and women entrepreneurs achieve 10X sales growth through live commerce, AI product listing, and free mini-websites.

Rachna has empowered 5,000+ women to start earning from home with zero investment. Her work has been recognised and supported by NSRCEL (IIM Bangalore), IGDTUW, GGSIPU, Chitkara University, and Sharda University.
